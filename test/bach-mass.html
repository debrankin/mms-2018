Bach Mass in B Minor

 

(Two performances)

Gendron Franco Center, Lewiston

his year the Chorale will present the
incredible Mass in B Minor by Johann Sebastian Bach. This magnum opus was composed over several decades and finally assembled by Bach near the end of his life.
The work has been widely hailed as one of the greatest compositions of all time. The Maine Music Society Chorale and Orchestra will perform this timeless piece by the most celebrated composer of the 18th century. Professional soloists will join the Chorale
