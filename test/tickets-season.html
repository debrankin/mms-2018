Box Office Policies & Discounts

Discounts for some concerts as listed

Our lower priced tickets are available to:
Full-time students with ID
Seniors 65 & older
Children 12 & under are free where noted

Children
Children under 12 are free where noted. All children must be accompanied by an adult. Please be considerate of other audience members.

Group Sales
We offer group discounts on individual shows not season tickets (those are discounted already). Group orders are through the office. Discount is generally between 15-20% though actual amount may vary at our discretion.

Late Seating

At the discretion of house manager. Ticketed seating is guaranteed up to ten minutes prior to curtain. Standing is not permitted in the theater.

Accessibility

All theaters are wheelchair accessible. Please call in advance to let us know if you require special seating.

 

By Mail: For Season Tickets, download and complete the order form and return it with your payment to: Maine Music Society, 221 Lisbon St., Lewiston, ME 04240. All tickets ordered by mail will be delivered by mail. 

By Phone: Season or Individual Concert Tickets may be purchased over the phone with a credit card. To order call our Box Office at 207-782-7228. Box Office hours are Mon- Fri 10 AM - 3 PM; closed holidays. Tickets ordered over the phone may be picked up at the venue, open one hour before curtain.

In Person: Individual Concert Tickets may be purchased at the door at the time of the performance for $2 more, if availability permits.
