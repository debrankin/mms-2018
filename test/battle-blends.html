Battle of the Blends XXII


Saturday, November 5, 2016 at 7:30 p.m.

The Gendron Franco Center, Lewiston


he Battle of the Blends is always a sell-out event. Our audiences have come to love this extraordinary gathering of a cappella talent. Groups from around New England gather to present a variety of a cappella music, ranging from madrigals to modern jazz and pop. This year’s groups include: None of the Above, the Crosstones, Northfield, Edward Little A Cappella Choir, the Afternotes and the Maine Music Society Chamber Singers.

 

Ticket prices: General Admission, $25 and children under 12, $12
