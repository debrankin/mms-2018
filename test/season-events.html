Battle of the Blends

Saturday, November 5, 2016 at 7:30 p.m.

The Gendron Franco Center, Lewiston

he Battle of the Blends is always a sell-out event. Our audiences have come to love thisextraordinary gathering of a cappella talent. Groups from around New England gather to present a variety of a cappella music, ranging from madrigals to modern jazz and pop. This year’s groups include: None of the Above, the Crosstones, Northfield, Edward Little A Cappella Choir, the Afternotes and the Maine Music Society Chamber Singers.

Bach Mass in B Minor

Saturday, March 25, 2017 at 7:30 p.m. Sunday, & March 26, 2017 at 3:00 p.m.

Gendron Franco Center, Lewiston

his year the Chorale will present the
incredible Mass in B Minor by Johann Sebastian Bach. This magnum opus was composed over several decades and finally assembled by Bach near the end of his life. The work has been widely hailed as one of the greatest compositions of all time. The Maine Music Society Chorale and Orchestra will perform this timeless piece by the most celebrated composer of the 18th century. Professional soloists will join the Chorale for this special presentation.

Songs of Carole King & James Taylor

Saturday, May 20, 2017 at 7:30 p.m.
Sunday, May 21, 2017 at 3:00 p.m.

The Gendron Franco Center, Lewiston

nce again the Chorale will celebrate the music  of two extraordinary popular composers. These musicians composed and performed so many favorites!
