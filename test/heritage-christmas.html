A Heritage Christmas

(Two performances)
Saturday, December 10, 2016 at 7:30 p.m. & Sunday, December 11, 2016 at 3:00 p.m

The Gendron Franco Center, Lewiston


A Heritage Christmas performed by the Maine Music Society Chorale has become a beloved Christmas tradition and will be a definite highlight of the season as we focus on Christmas music from the United States. You will hear such favorites as White Christmas,
I’ll Be Home for Christmas and It’s the Most Wonderful Time of the Year! But be on the alert for a few different twists with songs like The Twelve Days After Christmas - sure to tickle your funny bone. To top off this holiday season’s celebration, we will follow tradition with everyone joining in to sing

Ticket prices: adults $22, students $10, seniors $20, children under 12 free.
