Battle of the Blends XXII

Saturday, November 7, 2015 at 7:30 p.m.
The Franco Center, Lewiston

Battle of the Blends is always a sell-out event. Our audiences have come to love this extraordinary gathering of a cappella talent. Groups from around New England gather to present a variety of a cappella music, ranging from madrigals to modern jazz and pop. This year’s groups include Talk to the Hand, the Edward Little High School Show Choir, Good-time Sound, The Maine Music Society Chamber Singers, The Crosstones, and the Nor’easters Barbershop Chorus.

A Heritage Christmas

Saturday, December 12, 2015 at 7:30 p.m.
Sunday, December 13, 2015 at 3:00 p.m.
The Franco Center, Lewiston

A Heritage Christmas, performed by The Maine Music Society Chorale (formerly the Androscoggin Chorale), has become a beloved Christmas tradition and is certainly a highlight of the season. This year the Chorale will perform beautiful arrangements of Christmas music from the British Isles. We’ll also present the riotous Bob Chilcott setting of “The Twelve Days of Christmas” along with many other seasonal pieces, both familiar and new. To top off this holiday season’s celebration, everyone will join in singing several Christmas carols.

Missa Solemnis

Sunday, April 3, 2016 at 4:00 p.m.
Basilica of Ss. Peter and Paul, Lewiston

Several years ago, more than 1400 people filled the Basilica of Ss. Peter and Paul for a thrilling performance of the Brahms Requiem. Once again, Artistic Director John Corrie will assemble the voices of the Maine Music Society Chorale, Bates College Choir, Edward Little HS A Cappella Choir, Lewiston HS Chamber Choir, Basilica Schola along with the orchestras of MMS and Bates College to present this momentous Solemn Mass by the master of the Classical music period, Ludwig van Beethoven. This deeply moving piece is rarely performed because of the large size of the orchestra and the demanding scope of the music. The concert will be a community event not to be missed.

Piano Men

Saturday, May 9, 2015 at 7:30 p.m. &
Sunday, May 10, 2015 at 3:00 p.m.
The Franco Center, Lewiston

This year the Chorale will celebrate the music of these two extraordinarily popular composers. Everyone has favorites from these two and we will sing as many as we can. Our selections will include Piano Man, Honesty, and Just the Way You Are by Billy Joel and Candle in the Wind, I Guess That’s Why They Call It The Blues, and Don’t Let The Sun Go Down On Me by Elton John.
