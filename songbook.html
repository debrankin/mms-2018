Favorites from the Great American Songbook

Saturday, May 19, 2018 at 7:30 p.m.
Sunday, May 20, 2018 at 3:00 p.m.

The Gendron Franco Center, Lewiston

he Chorale’s spring concert will highlight the music of two of the most celebrated early 20th century composers: George Gershwin and Cole Porter. With titles like I Get a Kick Out of You, I’ve Got You Under My Skin, and Just One of Those Things by Cole Porter and They Can’t Take That Away From Me, S’Wonderful and Summertime by George Gershwin, everyone will want to sing along. It’s hard to believe that almost a century has gone by since these jazzy tunes were popular, but they never lose their charm. To be sure, everyone will be delighted by the songs of these spectacular
composers.

Ticket prices: adults $20, students $10, seniors $18, children under 12 free.
