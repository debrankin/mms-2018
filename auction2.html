Silent Auction

Saturday, Dec. 14, 2014 ~ 7:30pm
Sunday, Dec. 15, 2014 ~ 3:00pm

The Franco Center, Lewiston

Come downstairs before and after the concert as well as during intermission to do your holiday shopping with over 50 items and gift certificates to choose from. A few of the items are shown above plus many other hand-crafted and unusual items and services to bid on.

Bidding will close after the concert on Sunday afternoon, December 15 and you do not have to b present to claim your purchase..

Here are some items that are sure to interest you:

  • Four of John Corries' cheesecakes - yum!
  • Woodland walks in the Towle forest
  • Two boxes of approximately 1500 baseball cards each.
  • First six issues of Sports Illustrated
  • Gift certificats to be used at local merchants
  • Many collector's items
  • One week vacation at Islesboro cottage (a perennial favorite) see www.islesbororental.com
