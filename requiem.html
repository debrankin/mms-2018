Requiem

Saturday, March 28, 2015 at 7:30 p.m. &

Sunday, March 29, 2015 at 3:00 p.m.

The Franco Center, Lewiston

It will be such a profound joy to present these requiems by two French composers, Gabriel Fauré and Maurice Duruflé, whose works have moved audiences around the world. The two pieces are frequently paired in concert because they complement each other so well. The horns, trumpets, strings, harp, timpani, and organ of the Maine Music Society Orchestra will accompany the 70 voices of the Chorale. This concert will be an experience to remember.

Ticket prices: adults $20, seniors $18, students $10, & children under 12 free.
