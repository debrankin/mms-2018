Songs of Carole King & James Taylor

Saturday, May 20, 2017 at 7:30 p.m.
Sunday, May 21, 2017 at 3:00 p.m.

The Gendron Franco Center, Lewiston

nce again the Chorale will celebrate the music  of two extraordinary popular composers. These musicians composed and performed so many favorites! Selections of James Taylor songs will include How Sweet It Is To Be Loved By You, Sweet Baby James, and Fire and Rain. Selections of Carole King will include such wonderful songs as It’s Too Late, I Feel the Earth Move, and You’ve Got a Friend. To be sure, everyone will be delighted to hear once again songs by these spectacular composers.

Ticket prices: adults $20, students $10, seniors $18, children under 12 free.
