2016 - 2017 Performance Season

Greetings from
Artistic Director, John Corrie

t amazes me that I have been enjoying my work and music making with the Maine Music Society for ten years now.   Ten Years!!  Without a doubt the greatest pleasures of these past ten years have been meeting and working with so many wonderful people -- members of the Board of Directors as well as the singers and orchestra players with whom I make music.   All of them have taught me so much.  In addition to the people, I have discovered new music -- from the greatest of the master composers like Bach, Beethoven and Mozart to contemporary composers, including our own Colin Britt from Auburn, Maine. These discoveries have added depth to our concerts.
The exploration continues with new selections for our Heritage Christmas concert, the Bach B Minor Mass in March (which has been on my wish list for so long), and the season finale in May with songs by Carole King and James Taylor.  A season with music for everyone!
Come celebrate the joy of singing with us -- Making Music Soar!

Sincerely,



Artistic Director

Interview on Z105.5's Breakfast Club
